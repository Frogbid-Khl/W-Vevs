Create a website that runs like your real business and generate sales right away!

Stand out from the crowd with a perfectly organised website. Allow your consumers to quickly find the information they need and complete their purchases right away!

Site menu

Build a website with a stellar online appearance. To make it easier for you to organise it, you can choose from the premade top site and footer menu. Depending on your preferences, you may also add a drop-down menu with 1, 2 or 3 columns and effortlessly create an easy-to-navigate website.

Regional Settings

Set specific regional settings, such as currency, timezone, date & time, specific calendar options and dialling codes to make your client’s purchase journey straightforward. With VEVS Website Builder, you can make your website work as your real business does.

Maps

Deliver outstanding customer service by choosing from our four predefined map providers and adding it with your location on your VEVS website. Thus you will make it easier for your clients to find you.

Web forms

We know that the contact form is the “face” of your customer service. Here’s why you can personalise it by editing it to best suit your business model.

You can also add as many forms and pages with forms as you want. This could be, for instance, an application form, survey, feedback form, inquiry form, job application, quotation form, and more.

External code

Connect third-party accounts to your website, such as Facebook pixel, Google analytics, Google tags manager, or other tracking tools, to better manage successful marketing campaigns and make your business grow faster.

You can also use external functional and user-related software like online chats, widgets, etc.

Users & Roles

Do you want to bring your team together around your website and make it work as your real business does? You may provide them access to your website or only certain pages in a few simple steps with that option.

Blog

Create a blog and publish it on your website to always keep your consumers informed about your business innovations.

Add regularly fresh content to your website, connect with visitors and improve your SEO with ease.
