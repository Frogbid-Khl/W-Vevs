Online business made easy! Мove your real business to the online space.

Have ultimate control over your online business! Take a look at some of the basic features that our industry-specific software includes. Get detailed information about what more is contained on each industry page.

Online reservations & appointments

Provide a remarkable customer experience by letting your clients make reservations directly on your website. Have granular control over your rental business by accepting and managing the reservations thanks to our built-in industry-specific software. We've done the work so you can focus on what matters most - your business.

Online payments

With VEVS Website Builder, you save money. You don't have to pay for external software to accept and manage your online payments efficiently. Your website has everything! Your clients can pay securely for your products or services directly on your website. We provide you with 15+ payment gateways to choose from, and you can also request other online payment tools of your liking.

Inventory management

Conquer the online space with an easy-to-navigate website. Add and manage your inventory straightforwardly, depending on the business that you own. This could be cars, car parking spaces, shuttle & taxis, vacation properties, and more.

Availability management

Take advantage of the availability management in real-time, which most of our website solutions offer and avoid the headaches of overlapping bookings. Have complete control over your bookings, appointments and reservations directly on your VEVS website.

Reservations management

By making a VEVS website, you will have easy access to all the reservation data details. You can also edit the reservations and make changes if needed. That way, you’ll always stay up to date with how your online business performs.

Customer management

Cut down on paperwork by having an online archive directly on your website with all your customers who made a reservation. You can also export the customer data, use it in your email campaign and boost your digital marketing performance.

Automated email and SMS notifications

You no longer need to spend hours sending emails and SMS notifications manually. Everything is automated for you! Simply set it to your liking and start growing!
